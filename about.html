WHO WE ARE

VODAF.org is the official website for Help for Victims of Matrimonial Abuse Foundation. We are committed to reducing domestic violence to its barest minimum. The founders have at some point been victims and survivor of Intimate partner violence in the hands of their ex- spouses. All through the period, they sought help for several years and there was absolutely no help and support available. Because there was no help and support available for them and their small children, they had to do everything humanly possible for the safety their small children and they themselves.

Why Choose Us

Our intervention strategy focuses on being proactive and reactive. The our proactive strategy is aimed at enlightening the public about domestic violence, how to avoid it, and critical actions to take in the event of a live occurrence of domestic violence.

Committed Cohorts

We provide individuals/victims personal support to overcome the psychological, emotional and physical abusive situation that they are facing. We have monthly Men and Women’s Support Groups which runs separately.

Trained Facilitators

We provide Anger Management Treatments and Relationship Counselling to the female/male counterparts.

Access to Leading Voices

We escalate cases of domestic violence to relevant authorities as well as provide treatment and counselling for victims of domestic violence. We endeavour to work together with other agencies to tackle violence against parents/children.
