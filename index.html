Who We Are

VODAH.org is the official website for Help for Victims of Matrimonial Abuse Foundation. We are committed to reducing domestic violence to its barest minimum.

What We Do

We provide individuals/victims personal support to overcome the psychological, emotional and physical abusive situation that they are facing.

Read More

How We Can Help

We escalate cases of domestic violence to relevant authorities as well as provide treatment and counselling for victims of domestic violence.

Read More

Reach Us

If you are a man or woman experiencing domestic violence/abuse and looking for support, please call our 24-hour helpline.
